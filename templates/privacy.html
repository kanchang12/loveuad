Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2024

Privacy-First Design

loveUAD is built with privacy at its core. We use 17-digit anonymous codes instead of personal identifiers. All patient data is encrypted end-to-end.

Data Collection

We collect minimal data necessary for the service to function: medication schedules, health records, and AI chat history. All data is encrypted and linked only to anonymous codes.

Data Usage

Your data is used solely to provide dementia care support services. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

Security

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information.
