Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 21, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to LoveUAD ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal health information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

Important: LoveUAD is a medication management application designed to help dementia patients and their caregivers. We handle sensitive health information and take your privacy seriously.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Registration Data: First name, last name, age, gender
  • Authentication: Unique 17-digit patient code (anonymized identifier)
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version

2.2 Health Information

  • Medication Data: Medication names, dosages, frequencies, and scheduled times
  • Adherence Records: Medication intake history and patterns
  • Prescription Documents: Images of prescriptions uploaded via OCR scanning
  • Chat History: Conversations with AI assistant about dementia care

2.3 Usage Data

  • App interaction logs
  • Feature usage statistics
  • Error reports and crash data
  • Notification delivery status

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Primary Purposes

  • Medication Management: Store and manage medication schedules
  • Reminders: Send timely medication reminders via notifications
  • Caregiver Monitoring: Allow authorized caregivers to monitor adherence
  • AI Assistance: Provide dementia care information through AI chat
  • OCR Processing: Extract medication information from prescription images

3.2 Secondary Purposes

  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
  • Provide customer support
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Security

4.1 Privacy-First Architecture

  • Anonymous Codes: We use 17-digit codes instead of personally identifiable information
  • End-to-End Encryption: All data transmitted between app and server is encrypted
  • Secure Storage: Data stored on secure servers with industry-standard encryption
  • No Email/Phone Required: Registration doesn't require email or phone number

4.2 Access Controls

  • Patient data accessible only via unique patient code
  • Caregivers must have the patient's code to access their data
  • No centralized user database linking codes to identities

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal or health information to third parties.

5.2 Limited Sharing

We may share your information only in these circumstances:

  • Authorized Caregivers: With caregivers who have your patient code
  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help operate the app (hosting, analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or court order
  • Emergency Situations: To prevent serious harm or protect safety

5.3 Third-Party Services

  • Cloud Hosting: Render.com (servers located in US/EU)
  • AI Services: Google Gemini for AI chat functionality
  • OCR Processing: Google Cloud Vision API for prescription scanning

6. Your Rights

6.1 Access and Control

  • View Data: Access all your stored information through the app
  • Export Data: Download your medication data as CSV
  • Delete Data: Remove individual medications or entire account
  • Modify Data: Update medication information at any time

6.2 Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data retained as long as account is active
  • Inactive accounts: Data deleted after 24 months of inactivity
  • Deleted accounts: All data permanently removed within 30 days
  • Backup data: Removed from backups within 90 days

7. Permissions

7.1 Required Permissions

  • Internet: Connect to servers for data sync
  • Notifications: Send medication reminders
  • Alarms: Schedule exact-time medication alerts
  • Boot Receiver: Restore alarms after device restart

7.2 Optional Permissions

  • Camera: Scan prescription documents (only when using OCR feature)
  • Storage: Export medication data to CSV (only when exporting)

8. Children's Privacy

LoveUAD is designed for adults with dementia and their caregivers. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

9. International Users

LoveUAD is operated from the United Kingdom. If you access the app from outside the UK, your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the UK or other countries where our service providers operate.

9.1 GDPR Compliance (EU/UK Users)

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

10. Cookies and Tracking

The LoveUAD mobile app does NOT use cookies. We collect only essential usage data for app functionality and improvement.

11. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we make changes:

  • We will update the "Last Updated" date
  • We will notify you via in-app notification
  • Significant changes will require your consent
  • Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance

12. Healthcare Disclaimer

Important: LoveUAD is a medication reminder and management tool. It is NOT a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult your doctor or healthcare provider regarding medication questions or concerns.
