What is loveUAD?

Evidence-based guidance for dementia caregivers

loveUAD (Love You Always Dad) is an AI-powered mobile application in development to provide dementia caregivers with evidence-based guidance between clinical appointments. When families receive a dementia diagnosis, they typically wait 6-12 months for specialist support.

During this gap, caregivers manage complex situations with minimal professional guidance. loveUAD will provide 24/7 access to dementia-specific advice - describe your situation in plain language, receive stage-appropriate guidance cited from medical research.

Founded by Kanchan Ghosh in Leeds, UK. Built from personal caregiving experience with 16+ years of software development expertise.

Important: loveUAD is currently in development. While our privacy architecture is under NHS Digital evaluation, the application has not yet been confirmed or approved by the NHS. We are seeking pilot partnerships for clinical validation.
