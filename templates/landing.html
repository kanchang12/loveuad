What is loveUAD?

loveUAD (Love You Always Dad) is an AI-powered mobile application that provides dementia caregivers with evidence-based guidance between clinical appointments.

When families receive a dementia diagnosis, they typically wait 6-12 months for specialist support. During this gap, caregivers manage complex situations with minimal professional guidance.

loveUAD provides 24/7 access to dementia-specific advice - describing your situation in plain language, receiving stage-appropriate guidance cited from medical research.

£4.99 per month

Important: loveUAD is currently in development. While our privacy architecture is under NHS Digital evaluation, the application has not yet been confirmed or approved by the NHS. We are seeking pilot partnerships for clinical validation.
