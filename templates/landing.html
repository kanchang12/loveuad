What is loveUAD?

**loveUAD (Love You Always Dad)** is an **AI-powered mobile app** designed specifically for **dementia caregivers** - not patients. When you're managing your loved one's care at home, loveUAD gives you the tools professionals use.

**Why Caregivers Need This:** When families receive a dementia diagnosis, they typically wait 6-12 months for specialist support. During this gap, you're managing complex medication schedules, behavioral changes, and safety concerns with minimal professional guidance. **loveUAD fills that gap.**

**Key Benefit:** Get 24/7 access to dementia-specific advice - describing your situation in plain language, receiving stage-appropriate guidance cited from **17,775 medical research papers**.

loveUAD: Features for Caregivers

loveUAD is your essential AI-powered co-pilot, designed to manage the complexities of dementia care at home.

  • Medication Tracking: Scan prescriptions with your camera, set reminders, and log what's been taken. Essential for adherence.
  • Real-Time Family Sync: Multiple caregivers see the same calendar, medication logs, and updates instantly. **No more "did dad take his pills?" phone calls.**

  • Care Calendar & Adherence: See medication schedules, track adherence patterns over weeks/months, and share reports with family members or clinicians.
  • 100% Anonymous: No patient names or addresses stored anywhere - just a secure 17-digit connection code you share with trusted family.
