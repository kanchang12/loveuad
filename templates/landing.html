What is loveUAD?

loveUAD (Love You Always Dad) is an AI-powered mobile app designed specifically for dementia caregivers - not patients. When you're managing your loved one's care at home, loveUAD gives you the tools professionals use.

When families receive a dementia diagnosis, they typically wait 6-12 months for specialist support. During this gap, you're managing complex medication schedules, behavioral changes, and safety concerns with minimal professional guidance. loveUAD fills that gap.

Privacy & Security

×

Built on NHS-Evaluated Technology

loveUAD is built on an open-source privacy-first architecture currently under NHS Digital evaluation. Our technology has been published and peer-reviewed for healthcare use.

Zero Personal Data Collection

We never collect or store:

  • Patient names or identities
  • Home addresses or locations
  • Phone numbers or contact details
  • NHS numbers or medical IDs
  • Any personally identifiable information

How Anonymous Connection Works

When you create a care circle, you receive a unique 17-digit anonymous code. Share this code only with trusted family caregivers. They enter the code to instantly join your care coordination system - no registration, no personal details, completely anonymous.

End-to-End Encryption

All medication logs, care notes, and health data are encrypted from the moment you enter them until family members view them. Even loveUAD cannot see your family's medical information.

NHS-Compliant Infrastructure

Hosted on Google Cloud Platform in UK data centers, meeting NHS Digital security standards for healthcare applications.

Open Source Technology

Our privacy architecture is publicly documented and peer-reviewed. View the full technical documentation: https://zenodo.org/records/17440767

For Caregivers (Not Patients)

loveUAD is designed for family members managing a loved one's dementia care at home. Here's what you get:

📋 Medication Tracking

  • Camera Prescription Scanning: Point your phone at prescription labels - AI extracts medication name, dosage, and schedule automatically
  • Smart Reminders: Set custom alerts for each medication
  • Track What's Taken: Mark medications as given, view adherence history
  • Visual Calendar: See the entire week's medication schedule at a glance

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Real-Time Family Sync

  • Multiple Caregivers: Add unlimited family members using anonymous 17-digit codes
  • Instant Updates: When one caregiver logs a medication, everyone sees it immediately
  • No More Phone Tag: Stop texting "did dad take his pills?" - check the app instead
  • Shared Calendar: All caregivers see the same medication schedule and care notes

🤖 AI Health Guidance (17,775 Research Papers)

  • Ask in Plain English: "My mom refuses her evening medication, what should I do?"
  • Evidence-Based Answers: Get guidance backed by peer-reviewed dementia research
  • Stage-Appropriate Advice: Recommendations tailored to your loved one's dementia progression
  • 24/7 Availability: Get help at 2 AM when you can't reach a doctor

📊 Care Calendar & Analytics

  • Medication Adherence Tracking: See patterns over days, weeks, and months
  • Missed Dose Alerts: Identify problems before they become emergencies
  • Share with Doctors: Export reports to bring to medical appointments

🔒 100% Anonymous

  • No Names Stored: Patient identity never leaves your device
  • 17-Digit Connection Codes: Share care access without sharing personal info
  • End-to-End Encryption: Your family's medical data is completely private
